## Governance objective

Governance should protect velocity and trust at the same time.
The hybrid model does this by bootstrapping leadership through appointments, then
transitioning legitimacy to member elections.

## Leadership model

- Founder appoints initial chief for launch stability.
- Chief term has a fixed window and visible end date.
- Election opens before term expiry to avoid power gaps.
- All appointments and election outcomes are logged publicly.

## Election protocol

1. Candidate window opens with eligibility requirements.
2. Candidate list is locked before voting starts.
3. One verified member, one vote.
4. Vote closes at fixed timestamp and is finalized server-side.

## Abuse prevention rules

- No backdated role changes during open elections.
- Vote updates allowed until close, but only one active vote per member.
- Conflict-of-interest declarations required for moderators and candidates.

## Minimum cadence recommendation

- Elections every 3 months for high-activity clans.
- Confidence review at midpoint for inactive chiefs.
- Emergency appointment only for vacancy or severe misconduct.
